Project Brief
Build a new website for the client that:

Uses the style, layout, and feel of the “Education” page on the Halkin Mason Photography website (https://www.halkinmasonphotography.com/education
)

Incorporates all of the information from the current site of Anthony Ruiz Photography (https://www.anthonyruizphoto.com/
)

Design / Style Requirements

Clean, modern, gallery-first layout as seen on Halkin Mason’s “Education” page.

Large leading images, minimal distractions, strong visual emphasis.

Navigation structure similar to Halkin Mason: e.g., main menu at top, portfolio sections, about/contact.

Typography and spacing consistent with high-end photography sites (ample white space, large headings).

Use of high-quality photography thumbnails and full-width hero images.

Smooth transitions or hover effects for portfolio items (optional but recommended).

Mobile-responsive design, optimized for tablets and phones.

Content Requirements
From Anthony Ruiz Photography site extract and include:

Business name: Anthony Ruiz Photography

Tagline: Real Estate Media / Interior & Exterior Photography (and Aerial / Drone Video)

Services: Real estate photography, commercial real-estate photography, drone gallery, 4K video, Matterport/3D tours, twilight shoots, virtual twilights, retouching/HDR, etc. (as listed on the site)

FAQs section (questions like: How long shoot takes? What if weather isn’t ideal? When will the photos be ready? Can you shoot twilight? Do you offer editing/retouching? How far in advance to book?)

Call to Action: Book now / Contact us for a quote

Gallery sections: Real Estate Gallery, Commercial RE Gallery, Drone Gallery, 4K Video

Pricing page link (or page)

“About” or short business overview: mention “professional real estate photography and video services in South Florida” and “24 hour turnaround, competitive pricing, professional equipment and Part 107 certified” (as per the site)

Contact details / Booking link: e.g., “Book now” link to listings.anthonyruizphoto.com

Use of hero heading: “Images that Impact” (or something similar)

Use of example images/backgrounds similar to those shown on Anthony Ruiz site (poolside area, modern living room, aerial views) as placeholders or reference.

Structure / Pages
Suggested page structure (based on Halkin Mason + your content):

Home — hero image, tagline, short intro, key services, CTA “Book Now”

Services — separate sections for: Real Estate Photography, Commercial Real Estate, Drone & Aerial Video, 3D/Matterport Tours, Twilight/Virtual Twilight, Retouching & Editing

Gallery / Portfolio — categories: Real Estate Gallery, Commercial RE Gallery, Drone Gallery, 4K Video. Each category has thumbnail grid with light-box or link to full project.

Pricing — outline packages and/or custom quote option.

FAQs — provide the FAQ items above.

About — overview of the business, credentials (Part 107 certified drone pilot, 24-hour turnaround, South Florida focus)

Contact / Book — booking link, contact form or contact details.

Technical / Functional

Use a CMS or website builder that supports gallery grids, lightbox, responsive behavior.

Ensure fast performance (optimize images) and good SEO (descriptive headings, alt tags).

Include a clear bookings / contact CTA on every page (e.g., header or sticky button).

Analytics & tracking (Google Analytics or similar) for visitor behavior.

Accessibility best practices (alt text, contrast, keyboard navigation).

Optional: integrate Matterport or similar 3D tour links if available.

Deliverables

Wireframes/mockups for desktop and mobile views.

Full site with all above pages, content transferred and styled.

Image assets optimized and properly placed.

Working booking/contact functionality.

Deployment to client’s domain with proper SSL certificate.

Training or documentation for client to update gallery, add new images.
